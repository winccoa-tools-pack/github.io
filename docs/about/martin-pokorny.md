
## 👋 About Me

**Martin Pokorný**  
Started the winccoa-tools-pack project  
Senior Key Developer & Key QA Engineer (WinCC OA)

I started the winccoa-tools-pack project with a simple intention:  
to improve the developer and QA experience in WinCC OA environments by creating  
modern tooling, CI/CD integrations, automation patterns, and shared best practices.

My work spans development, QA, test automation, and workflow engineering.  
I enjoy building tools that reduce manual effort, increase consistency,  
and make everyday engineering tasks more predictable and enjoyable.

Currently, I’m exploring new ideas such as **OALint**  
(a future linting and rule‑checking concept for WinCC OA projects and not only ctrl scripts)  
and **OAPM**, an early concept for **WinCC OA project management tooling**  
inspired by NPM-style workflows — to help developers organize, share,  
version, and maintain WinCC OA project components more easily.

While I initiated the project, it has already evolved beyond just my own work.  
The ecosystem grows through the contributions, feedback, and testing  
from many helpful people — and I’m grateful for everyone who participates
